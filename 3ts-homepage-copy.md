## WHAT WE DO

Our work is organized around four engagements, each designed for a different stage of the alignment problem.

### Alignment Read

A two-week senior diagnostic. Confidential interviews with the principals and key stakeholders, a map of where alignment is breaking, and a sponsor memo on what is actually at stake and what should happen next. Often the first engagement.

### Decision Sprint

A four-to-six-week engagement built around a specific stalled decision or consequential convening. Stakeholder preparation, decision architecture, the room itself, and a thirty-day decision tracker once the alignment is reached.

### Retained Advisory

Ongoing senior advisory through the life of a transformation, reform, or governance initiative. Confidential support for the sponsor, repeated convenings, principal coaching, and quiet judgment between the formal moments.

### First 90 Days

Three months of confidential support for newly-appointed senior leaders stepping into politically sensitive roles. Stakeholder mapping, sponsor coaching, and judgment through the early decisions that shape the rest of the tenure.

## NOTES FOR DESIGN HANDOFF

**Tone register:** Operator-level, private-advisor, evidence-led, restrained. Few adjectives, specific nouns, clear consequences. The page should feel like a private briefing note from a senior advisor, not a marketing site.

**Hierarchy:** Hero sets the category. Who We Work With filters. What We Do shows the offering ladder. Selected Engagements is the proof — with the confidentiality framing doing authority + scarcity work in one line. About is brief and credentials-led. Testimonials are restrained, three only. Contact is friction-positive (request, not book).

**Discretion is a feature, not a constraint.** The "Most of our work is confidential" line should land with visual weight. Empty space around it. It does more work than any of the proof blocks.

**Things deliberately not on the homepage:** Pricing, service-buffet language, calendar widget, ideological service lines, hero-level mission statement. All by design.
